Towards the ubiquitous Citizen Data Scientist

Having worked in the Search/NLP/ML and Data Science spaces for the last decade, we got to understand how tedious and costly preparing datasets for a data science project can be.

Many of the nice off-the-shelf tools make data manipulation easier. But to acquire large amounts of data, analyze it with NLP, query it and prep it for consumption in other systems, options seem to be limited to enterprise solutions. Even with enterprise software, implementing a smart AI initiative is lenghty, costly and difficult to maintain.

At relevance.io, we obsess over delivering AI tools similar to those available to enterprise buyers, while making them affordable and easy-to-use. We want data scientists to be empowered to do what they love the most: crunching data! Long live the Citizen Data Scientist!

Human-centric

Human interaction is pivotal to make quality ML models: we make sure everything is built-in to facilitate the annotation of training datasets.

The best apps are simple and straight forward: we design user experiences that simplify acquisition, analysis, curation, delivery and annotation of data.
